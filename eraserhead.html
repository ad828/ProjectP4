Eraserhead

Eraserhead

Not much can really be said about Eraserhead that would make much of any sense. David Lynch’s experimental body horror opus tells a story of Henry, a man who tries to survive in a bleak, brutalist industrial hellscape, while dealing with constant unbearable screams of his mutant child. This film defies anything that you’ve seen before, as we wade through viscous liquid metal exteriors and try to make some sense of anything that is happening to our everyman protagonist. As with most films run here, it initially opened to small audiences however gained prominence as a midnight movie.
