Upcoming Events

Q&A with American Psycho director Mary Harron

For this pivotal film's 20th anniversary, director Mary Harron gives her insights into how to make powerful films that stand the test of time. She will be taking questions from 6:00-7:00 PM with a special showing at 8:00. A DJ will be there playing the film's incredible soundtrack with tracks like “Hip to be Square” and “Sussudio” and “True Faith” in between the Q & A and the screening.

Rocky Horror Picture Show Shadowcast

The incredibly awesome cast of RHPS NYC are coming to our theater to perform a shadowcast of Rocky Horror! If you have ever seen them before, they play in various theaters across New York and are insanely talented. They will be acting along with the film playing above them and provides a unique and entertaining spin on this cult classic. Be sure to book tickets in advance as tickets will absolutely sell out!

DJ set with Wendy Carlos 15 Year Anniversary

Wendy Carlos’ 15 Anniversary since her last soundtrack was produced. A transgender icon and producer of some of the greatest film soundtracks of all time, We are paying respect to her legacy with a DJ set of some of her music. Songs include music from A clockwork orange, the shining, tron, and woundings. As well as some of her solo career music like “Switched-On Bach” and “Well Tempered Synthesizer” Admission starts at 10$ with a 2 drink minimum.
